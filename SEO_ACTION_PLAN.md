## Success Metrics

Track these in Google Search Console:
- **Impressions** - How many times you appear in search
- **Clicks** - How many people click your link
- **CTR** - Click-through rate (aim for 3-5%)
- **Average Position** - Current ranking (aim for top 3)

Target Goals (6 months):
- 500+ monthly impressions from local keywords
- 50+ monthly clicks to website
- Rank #1 for "moving services [city]" variations
- 10+ Google Business Profile reviews

❌ Don't buy backlinks - focus on organic links
❌ Don't ignore mobile optimization (already done!)
